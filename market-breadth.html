Signal-MBI — 6 breadth boxes (4R, 4R-chg, 20R, 20R-chg, 50R, 50R-chg) ka sum, -6 se +6 tak. ≥+3 = Bullish day, ≤-3 = Bearish day, beech me Neutral (ya 3+ red boxes ho par overall red na ho to "Neutral (Warning)").
Net Breadth — (Stocks up 4%+) minus (Stocks down 4%+). Positive matlab buyers hावी.
Net NH-NL — Naye 52-week highs minus naye 52-week lows. Market ki underlying strength/weakness dikhata hai.
% Above EMA (10/20/50/200) — Kitne % stocks apne respective moving average ke upar trade kar rahe hain — short se long-term participation ka health-check.
% Within 15% of 52W High — Kitne % stocks apne 52-week high ke qareeb (85%+) hain — leadership ka breadth.
MBI Trend / Divergence — Aaj ka Signal-MBI 10 din pehle se compare karke "Improving/Weakening" batata hai, aur agar Nifty up hote hue bhi MBI weak ho raha hai (ya vice-versa) to Divergence flag hota hai — early warning signal.
